Tradition

At Mexican Fiesta, we have proudly served delicious authentic Mexican food for the people of Metro Detroit for over 40 years.We are proud of our family and our reputation, and hope it shows in our food and service.

History

Sam and Francis Alvarado's dream started in a small location located in Dearborn Heights back in 1963. With a lot of hard work, that location steadily grew and so did the Alvarado family. Three generation and two locations later, we are still here providing the same great authentic mexican food that many people have grown to love.
